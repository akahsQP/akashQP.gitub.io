About Me

Akash Shaha, under the caring guidance of Gobinda Chandra Shaha, was born and raised in Sirajganj, Bangladesh. In 2014, he completed his Secondary School Certificate (S.S.C) from Porjana M.N High School, achieving a remarkable CGPA of 5.00 out of GPA-5. His academic journey continued as he pursued a Diploma in Electrical Engineering at Pabna Polytechnic Institute, graduating in 2018 with a stellar CGPA of 3.84 out of GPA-4, and earning selection for a stipend in recognition of his academic excellence. In 2019, Akash embarked on the next phase of his education, enrolling at Dhaka University of Engineering & Technology, Gazipur, to pursue his graduation. Alongside his studies, Akash honed his practical skills in various areas of electrical engineering, including CT, PT, Electrical Circuits, and Electronic Circuits. He demonstrated proficiency in a multitude of software applications and acquired hands-on experience with electronic components and machinery. Akash's academic achievements were complemented by his active participation in extracurricular activities and community service. He held various leadership roles in student welfare associations, contributed to religious publications, and gained teaching experience across different educational levels. Notably, he secured the 2nd position in the Hult Prize Idea Competition and showcased his leadership skills as a Senior Organizer of the DUET EEE Fest-2K22.
